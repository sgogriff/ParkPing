# ParkPing Privacy

ParkPing keeps things simple: your data stays on your phone. There is no ParkPing account, no cloud sync, and no ParkPing server lurking in the background collecting your parking data.

## Data stored locally

- Place names
- Place coordinates and geofence radii
- Optional Wi-Fi SSIDs linked to places
- Reminder text, snooze duration, and language preference
- Daily reminder state, including trigger time, snooze state, completion state, and monitoring status

## Data not collected

- No account data
- No cloud sync
- No analytics
- No advertising or identifiers
- No exported location history
- No parking reminder data sent to the developer

___
___
ParkPing does not need a 'backend' to do its job.

The app only uses internet access to load OpenStreetMap tiles for the preview map in Setup.

ParkPing does not upload anything to anywhere.

Android / Google Play services may still use normal platform connectivity to support geofencing and other OS features, but thats an Android thing!

Depending on your phone and Google backup settings, Android may include app data in device backups. That should only be accessible to you.

Uninstalling the app removes ParkPing from the device and clears all its local app data on the phone.
